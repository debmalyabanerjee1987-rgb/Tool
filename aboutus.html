About ToolKit-DMB

Welcome to ToolKit-DMB, your all-in-one, browser-based toolkit to quickly compress images, convert PDFs, edit files, and more—all for free and with no installations or sign-ups required.

Whether you're optimizing images for the web, merging PDFs, or doing light file editing, our tools are designed for speed, simplicity, and privacy. Everything runs locally in your browser—your files never leave your device.

Our Story

ToolKit-DMB was created to streamline common digital tasks without clutter. Rather than booking apps or downloads, what if you could just open your browser and do what you need—fast and hassle-free? That’s how ToolKit-DMB was born.

Created and maintained by Deb Malya Banerjee, every aspect is thoughtfully designed to give you maximum utility with minimal fuss.
